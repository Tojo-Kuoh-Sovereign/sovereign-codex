# Purpose

The Quality Statement defines the purpose of quality assurance throughout the Sovereign Documentation Framework.

Its purpose is to ensure that documentation consistently meets the governance, research, and standards established by the framework while supporting continuous improvement over time.

---

# Statement

Quality is the process of verifying that documentation fulfils its intended responsibility.

Rather than creating additional rules, Quality confirms that Governance, Research, and Standards have been applied consistently throughout the Sovereign Documentation Framework.

Quality exists to improve trust, maintainability, and long-term sustainability by encouraging objective review, constructive feedback, and continual refinement.

Quality should support improvement rather than simply identify defects.

---

# Principles

## Consistency

Quality should verify that documentation is implemented consistently throughout the framework.

---

## Objectivity

Reviews should be based on documented standards and evidence rather than personal preference.

---

## Traceability

Quality findings should be traceable to the relevant Governance documents, Research, or Standards wherever practical.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Quality is an ongoing process.

Review outcomes should be used to improve both individual documents and the framework itself.

---

## Practicality

Quality processes should remain proportionate to the value they provide.

Reviews should improve documentation without introducing unnecessary administrative overhead.

---

# Relationships

Quality supports the implementation of:

- Governance
- Research
- Standards
- Design
- Knowledge

Quality activities include:

- Document Reviews
- Accessibility Audits
- Navigation Reviews
- Release Readiness Reviews
- Quality Checklists

---

# Governance

Quality should remain independent from the creation of documentation wherever practical.

Separating implementation from review encourages objective assessment while supporting transparency and continual improvement.
