# Iconography

The Iconography system defines how icons are used throughout Sovereign Codex.

Its purpose is to improve recognition, navigation, and comprehension while remaining consistent with the platform's visual identity.

Icons should communicate meaning quickly without distracting from the information they support.

---

## Purpose

Icons exist to reinforce understanding.

They should help readers identify actions, concepts, and navigation more efficiently while remaining secondary to written content.

Whenever possible, text should remain the primary method of communication.

---

## Iconography Philosophy

Icons should be:

- Purposeful
- Recognisable
- Consistent
- Accessible
- Minimal

An icon should support understanding rather than replace it.

---

## Role of Icons

Icons should be used to:

- Support navigation
- Identify actions
- Reinforce information hierarchy
- Improve visual scanning
- Communicate familiar concepts

Icons should never be used purely for decoration.

---

## Consistency

Icons should maintain a consistent visual style throughout the platform.

Size, weight, spacing, and placement should remain predictable regardless of where an icon appears.

Consistency improves recognition and reduces cognitive effort.

---

## Labels

Icons should complement text rather than replace it.

Where meaning may be unclear, icons should be accompanied by descriptive labels.

Readers should never be expected to interpret unfamiliar icons without context.

---

## Accessibility

Icons should never be the sole method of communicating important information.

Meaning should always be reinforced through accompanying text, labels, or other visual cues.

Accessible iconography improves usability for every reader.

---

## Restraint

Icons should be introduced only when they improve understanding or navigation.

Excessive icon usage creates visual noise and reduces the effectiveness of the icons that genuinely communicate meaning.

Every icon should earn its place.

---

## Continuous Improvement

The Iconography system should evolve through careful refinement while maintaining consistency with the broader Design Language.

New icons should strengthen clarity, usability, and recognition without introducing unnecessary complexity.
