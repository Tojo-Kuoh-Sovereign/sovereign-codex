# Purpose

The Quality Statement defines the purpose of quality assurance throughout the Sovereign Documentation Framework.

Its purpose is to verify that documentation consistently satisfies the Governance, Research, Principles, and Standards established by the framework while supporting continuous improvement over time.

---

# Statement

Quality is the process of verifying that documentation fulfils its intended responsibility.

Rather than creating additional rules, Quality confirms that Governance, Research, Principles, Standards, and implementation have been applied consistently throughout the Sovereign Documentation Framework.

Quality exists to strengthen trust, maintainability, transparency, and long-term sustainability through objective review, constructive feedback, and continual refinement.

Quality should support improvement rather than simply identify defects.

---

# Principles

## Consistency

Quality should verify that documentation is implemented consistently throughout the framework.

---

## Objectivity

Reviews should be based on documented standards, evidence, and defined responsibilities rather than personal preference.

---

## Traceability

Quality findings should remain traceable to the relevant Governance, Research, Principles, Standards, or implementation decisions wherever practical.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Quality is an ongoing process.

Review outcomes should improve both individual documents and the framework itself.

---

## Practicality

Quality processes should remain proportionate to the value they provide.

Reviews should improve documentation without introducing unnecessary administrative overhead.

---

# Quality Activities

Quality activities may include:

- Document Reviews
- Accessibility Reviews
- Navigation Reviews
- Release Readiness Reviews
- Standards Compliance Reviews
- Quality Checklists

The scope and depth of review should remain appropriate to the significance of the work being evaluated.

---

# Relationships

Quality supports the implementation of:

- Governance
- Research
- Knowledge Presentation Principles
- Standards
- Design
- Knowledge

Quality verifies the consistent application of the Sovereign Documentation Framework rather than replacing Governance or implementation responsibilities.

---

# Governance

Quality should remain independent from the creation of documentation wherever practical.

Separating implementation from review promotes objective assessment, transparency, and continual improvement.

Quality processes should evolve only where improvements strengthen consistency, maintainability, or confidence in the framework.
