Research Topic
Navigation & Wayfinding
Research Question

How do people maintain orientation while navigating complex information?

Purpose

Investigate the factors that help people understand where they are, where they can go, how they arrived there, and how to efficiently navigate towards relevant information without becoming disoriented.

Scope

This research investigates how people navigate information spaces rather than physical spaces.

Topics include:

Maintaining orientation within a knowledge system.
Mental models of information structure.
Navigation cues.
Breadcrumbs and contextual navigation.
Hierarchical navigation.
Progressive exploration.
Reducing disorientation.

The objective is to understand the underlying concepts that enable effective navigation regardless of platform or technology.
