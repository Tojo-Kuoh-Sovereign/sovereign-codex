# Governance Principles

The Governance Principles establish the fundamental values that guide the development and maintenance of Sovereign Codex.

Every governance decision, documentation standard, and editorial process should support these principles.

---

## Accuracy

Information should be as accurate as reasonably possible at the time it is published.

Where uncertainty exists, it should be clearly identified rather than omitted or presented as fact.

---

## Consistency

Documentation should follow consistent structures, terminology, and presentation.

Consistency improves readability, reduces ambiguity, and makes the knowledge base easier to maintain over time.

---

## Transparency

Editorial decisions, assumptions, and evidence should be documented wherever appropriate.

Readers should be able to understand how conclusions were reached and what information supports them.

---

## Maintainability

Documentation should be written with long-term maintenance in mind.

Clear organisation, reusable standards, and structured information reduce the effort required to keep documentation current as the game evolves.

---

## Accessibility

Information should be presented in a way that is approachable for both new and experienced players.

Complex subjects should be explained clearly without unnecessarily increasing technical complexity.

---

## Community

Constructive feedback and contributions are encouraged.

While Sovereign Codex is curated to maintain quality and consistency, community involvement plays an important role in identifying improvements and expanding the knowledge base.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Documentation is never considered complete.

As Black Desert Online evolves, Sovereign Codex should continue to improve through research, verification, review, and refinement.
