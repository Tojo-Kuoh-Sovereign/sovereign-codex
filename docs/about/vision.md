# Project Vision

Sovereign Codex is intended to become a comprehensive and trusted knowledge base for Black Desert Online.

The long-term vision is to create documentation that is organised, searchable, easy to navigate, and maintained to a consistent standard, allowing players to quickly find reliable information without searching through multiple guides, videos, or Discord discussions.

As the project evolves, the Codex will continue to expand while maintaining the principles of accuracy, clarity, and consistency established by its governance and documentation standards.

---

## Long-Term Objectives

### Build a trusted reference

Develop documentation that players can confidently rely on for accurate and well-presented information.

### Improve accessibility

Present complex game systems in a structured way that makes them easier to understand for both new and experienced players.

### Support continuous improvement

Treat the Codex as a living project that evolves alongside Black Desert Online through ongoing research, verification, and refinement.

### Encourage community participation

Welcome constructive feedback, corrections, and ideas that help improve the quality and accuracy of the knowledge base.

### Establish a sustainable platform

Create documentation that is designed to remain maintainable over the long term through consistent governance, standards, and information architecture.

---

## Looking Ahead

While the initial focus is on Ocean content, the architecture has been designed to support a much broader knowledge base over time.

Future areas may include additional game systems, progression guides, life skills, equipment, mechanics, and other verified technical documentation as the project continues to grow.

The vision is not simply to build a collection of guides, but to establish a long-term resource that the community can rely upon for years to come.
