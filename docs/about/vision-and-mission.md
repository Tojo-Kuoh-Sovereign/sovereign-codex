# Vision & Mission

## Vision

To build a documentation ecosystem where knowledge is trusted, discoverable, and continuously improved.

## Mission

Transform information into trusted knowledge through governance, structure, and intelligent tools.

---

## Long-Term Goals

- Build documentation that remains valuable for years.
- Establish clear governance and quality standards.
- Reduce duplicated knowledge.
- Encourage contribution without sacrificing consistency.
- Create reusable systems that scale beyond a single project.

---

## Success Looks Like

Success is measured not by the number of pages written, but by the trust people place in the information they read.
